INDIA

  1. There Are a Lot Of Guesthouses

    It's normal to sleepover with an Indian family and even explore their country with them -like a local. Look through the web for sites to reach them.

  2. Dress to Respect

    Indian culture isn't particularly like yours, so remember to dress respectably and not revealing, to save yourself from unwanted harassment or comments that you might not like.

  3. You’ll Be Treated like a Celebrity

    Yes , they will easily identify you as a tourist and most probably ask for a picture or stop you.

  4. Don’t Walk Into Someone's House with Your Shoes On

    It's normal for streets to be filled with dirt and trash. Most Indians won’t like having your dirty shoe in their sacred homes.

  5. Book Train Travel As Much As 3 Months In Advance

    India is a crowded cit. From residents to tourists, everybody needs transportaion. You don't wanna be stuck in a place because you can't find a train to take you. Plan ahead!

  6. Don’t Use Your Left Hand for Anything

    Using your left hand is considered mean in India. Yes, we know it’s weird but cultures do vary...

  7. Have Reasonable Standards for Cleanliness

    Not all countries are as clean as your country so don’t have really high expectations and the cultural shock should be mild.

  8. Give As Much Tips As You Can

    Inidans like to be tipped. So leaving the change will be much appreciated.

  9. Do Not Pet Stray Dogs

    India is a dog-unfriendly country. Plus, hygiene concerns should make you keep your hands in those pockets.

  10. Don’t Except To Find Hair Dressers, Bring You Hair Essentials Girls

    In a country where people naturally have a silky flattering hair, they won’t need a hair dresser as much as you do , so have your essentials ready.
