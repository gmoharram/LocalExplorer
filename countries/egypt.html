EGYPT



  1. There Is Much More To Egypt Than Pyramids

    Guess what! Egypt do have Greek , Islamic and modern attraction also Egyptian beaches rank among the best beaches in the world, so yes there are so many things to do

  2. Assess the Add-Ons

    In Egypt taxes do count up as much as 25% of the original price, so you won’t really pay the price in your menu.

  3. Carry Change

    Always carry change with you. Sometimes the 1 Pound Bill is even more valuable than 100 EG

  4. Cross The Streets With Confidence

    In a crowded city it's normal that people cross huge streets quite often, don’t except somneone to wait for you. Traffic lights are often more decoration (albeit ugly ones) than serving to create order. So take a deep breath and go, go, go!

  5. Respect The Dress Code And Culture

    In the cities/rural areas you need a cover and wear respectable clothes. However, in other resorts it would be fine to wear summer clothes.

  6. Egypt Is Always Active

    Unlike other cities, in Egypt it’s never too late nor to early, you will always find something to do and people around. Eating out at 1 am is a common friday activity.

  7. Expect Diversity

    There are so many types of people in Egypt from party animals to meditation angels, and religious people, you can always find people to hang with , just search.

  8. Very Distinctive Cuisine

    Egyptians do respect food which leads to a great variety, make sure to try them , but first know what you can handle and what you can't.

  9. Don’t pay in a Foreign Currency

    Currency rates are so variable in Egypt, so make sure to pay in their Egyptian pound in order not to be exploited by any mean.

  10. Expect some attention

    Walking throught he streets you might grab the attention of a group of kids teliing you hello and asking how you are doing. They might even insist on practicing their French skills on you - whether you speak French or not.
