SPAIN

  1. Don’t Say Adios

    Weird Right? But, Seriously The Word “Adios” Is Not Used In Spain. Generally, You Say “Hasta Luego” Or “Ciao.” Saying “Adios” Is The Equivalent Of Saying “Goodbye I’m A Tourist.

  2. Don’t Call Barcelona Spain

    Just DONT, through history conflicts between Barcelona and Spain had always been on fire , it’s better not to get engaged in their internal issues.

  3. Take Advantage of Public Transport

    Public transportation in Spain , is really clean, affordable and easy to access.

  4. Spaniards Don't Eat When You Normally Do

    10:00 Pm is the normal lunch time in Spain, because of their laidback attitude.

  5. Easter Is More Important Than Christmas

    Easter is considered the main celebrations week in Spain, which made spring celebrations in Spain so popular. Not only due to their religious background, but also due to the fiestas they have.

  6. While Violent Crime Is Very Low, Pick Pocketing Can and Does Happen

    You gotta watch your pockets bags and everything you carry around. Better wear that backpack the other way around!

  7. Don’t Just Visit The Main Cities

    Spain has more than just Barcelona and Madrid. Their rich culture and amazing shores are something that you don't want to miss out on.

  8. Flights Can Be Cheaper Than Trains

    Each county has its own rate but in Spain flights are really affordable.

  9. Eat Where Locals Eat

    Ask people around and find out where locals eat to enjoy your experience to the max.

  10. Don’t forget about the Paellas

    Don't need no explanation.
